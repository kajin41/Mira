About MiraSaves

Founded with the intention of creating innovative, comprehensive treatment for an array of mental illnesses, MiraSaves is developing our first virtual assistant to aid the recovery of people suffering from PTSD. Our technology will provide interactive assistance for the dissociative symptoms of the disorder, a smart log of day-to-day symptoms, and share information about a userâ€™s symptoms with their own psychologist to enhance in session treatment.
